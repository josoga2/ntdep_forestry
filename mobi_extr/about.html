From the Director

Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs) are a diverse group of parasitic, bacterial, fungal, viral and non communicable diseases that affect over 1 billion persons across Africa.
In Nigeria, over 1 million cases are being managed by the NTDEP.
On the basis of treatment options, NTDs are classified broadly into two groups:
- Preventive Chemotherapy NTDs;
- Innovative Intensified Disease Management NTDs
